Ingredients:
4 skin-on salmon fillets (6-8 oz. ea.)
Salt and ground black pepper
1/4 cup maple syrup
4 T butter, melted
2 T Dijon mustard
2 t apple cider vinegar
1 t Worcestershire sauce
Grease grill grate with brushed oil. Preheat to med-high.

Generously sprinkle salmon with salt and pepper.  
In small bowl, whisk together syrup, butter, mustard, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and 1 t red pepper flakes to make glaze.  
Brush salmon tops generously with glaze and place skin side down on grill.
Cook about 4 min. and flip until cooked to taste.

(adapted from Paula Deen)
